Lakmé 9to5 Primer + Matte is the first lip color with built-in primer for a long lasting, matte finish. The Built-in primer makes the color stay comfortably for 12 long hours. Lakmé 9to5 Primer + Matte gives a smooth and creamy finish which doesn’t make the lips crease. Moreover, you don’t require touch-ups for not 8 but 12 hours. The Key Primer Ingredient ensures that the lipstick gives a smooth finishs and feels extremely comfortable on your lips. Lakmé 9to5 Primer + Matte goes very well with Lakmé 9to5 Weightless Mousse Foundation as the base, followed by Lakmé 9to5 Flawless Compact.
